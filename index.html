FresNO? More like FresYES!

Fresno offers much to do when it comes to getting outside. I've compiled a few of my favorite outdoor locations found within Fresno County.

Outdoor Fun

Hover over each image to explore what outdoor adventures Fresno County has to offer!

Million Dollar Trail

This trail is named from an old mining area nearby. Fittingly this trail offers million dollar views of the San Joaquin River! With limited shade make sure to start this trail early during the summer! This photo was taken 2.0 miles from the trailhead but the trail offers stunning views throughout.

Stevenson Falls

This trail is named from an old mining area nearby. Fittingly this trail offers million dollar views of the San Joaquin River! With limited shade make sure to start this trail early during the summer! This photo was taken 2.0 miles from the trailhead but the trail offers stunning views throughout.

Tule Lake

Located just past Huntington lake this lake offers a chance to get away for the day. It is less well known when compared to larger lakes such as Shaver or Millerton Lake but the trip to get there is rewarded with calm and cool waters.

San Joaquin River Gorge

Located 1 hour outside of Fresno this hiking trail is rated as beginner/moderate. This trail offers beautiful views of the San Joaquin River. The Hike to the river is 0.4 miles long but the trail extends 14 miles for those who are brave!

China Peak

Fresno County's premiere ski and winter vacation spot. China Peak is locateed only 65 miles northeast of Fresno. Winter snowsports not your thing? No problem, China Peak offers mountain biking during the summer and the Indian Pools Trailhead is nearby.
